PROGRAMMER & COMPUTER LOVER...

I am fascinated by computers. It is crazy, at least to me, that we can fit millions of little logic gates in a silicon wafer that then can be used to simulate the universe, nature, people, the economy, anything in general that exists. Programming was one of the ways I got to use that power beginning in middle school making tic-tac-toe and a calculator. Now, using Scala I've published a paper about a simulation of Photometry on Saturn's rings. I regularly program from usage to personal projects, like this site or my web apps, to scientific research or mathematical problems.
